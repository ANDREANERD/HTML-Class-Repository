Case Study: Amazon Deforestation and Fauna Watch
The real Amazon

The Amazon rainforest plays a crucial role in regulating the world’s climate

3,358 Fires

A historic day of Amazon burning was detected by satellite monitoring. On 22 August, 3,358 fires were detected in the Brazilian Amazon. It was was the highest number of fires recorded for any 24-hour period since 2007.

Biodiversity & Human Cost

Deforestation and fires are linked. They are intertwined threats to the lives and livelihoods of Indigenous Peoples and traditional communities in the Amazon, to the biodiversity of the forest, and to the global climate
